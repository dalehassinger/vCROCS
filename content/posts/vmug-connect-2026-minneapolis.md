### The Path to Influence: From VMUG Attendee to vExpert, Blogger, and Conference Speaker

**Session Details:**
If you've ever felt the urge to present at a VMUG or start a technical blog but hesitated, this session is for you. At my first VMUG event, I was asking myself, "Can I do this?" My story is about setting a goal and finding fulfillment in the vCommunity by operating under one simple metric: If even a single person learns something new, the effort was worthwhile. Hear the personal story behind my decision to give back, the steps I took, and how you can start your own journey of presenting and contributing.

### Reflections on VMUG Connect  

**I recently attended VMUG Connect in Minneapolis. Here are the key takeaways from the event:**

- **Location:** Minneapolis is a practical central hub. The city is clean, transit from the airport to the venue is efficient, and the local dining options are excellent.

- **Content:** The technical sessions were well-executed. Presenters covered relevant topics that resonated with the vCommunity.

- **Certification:** A major highlight was the onsite testing. VMUG Advantage members could sit for certification exams at no cost.

- **Overall Value:** From the food to the networking, the event was well-organized. I look forward to attending future sessions in this series.

- **vCommunity:** The event provided an opportunity to engage with the global VMUG community and share technical perspectives with members worldwide.    

- **Hands On Labs:** I enjoy leading the Expert-Led Workshops (ELW) within the Hands-on Labs (HOL) at the VMware conferences. While the standard HOL platform is available 24/7 online, these live sessions offer more than the standard modules. Attending an ELW allows us to dive into specific technical questions and explore custom scenarios in person.

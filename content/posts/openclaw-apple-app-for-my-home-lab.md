## **Exploring the Potential of OpenClaw**

My journey with OpenClaw began by deploying it as a VM, but I transitioned the setup to a Mac Mini once I realized its full potential. My goal was to thoroughly test the platform before dedicating a Mac Mini as a permanent OpenClaw server, specifically to leverage macOS-specific capabilities like Apple Reminders and Apple Notes.

While it is difficult to quantify exactly why, OpenClaw consistently outperforms standalone instances of ChatGPT or Claude when it comes to prompt execution. The level of detail and the quality of the results it returns are exceptional.

I have already integrated several custom skills into the environment. Most impressive is the "skill-building" skill—it is truly transformative. Currently, my setup includes connectors for VMware vCenter, VCF Operations, and Network Switches, with many more in development.
